I am Dr. Rishav Bansal, currently residing in Jaipur, Rajasthan.
I am a Consultant Geriatrician (Senior Citizens’ Specialist Physician). Geriatrics or Geriatric Medicine is a specialty that focuses exclusively on the healthcare of Senior Citizens (60 years of age and above), dealing with all the medical problems commonly faced by the elderly.
Geriatricians are doctors who have special knowledge of the aging process and special skills in the diagnosis, treatment, prevention & rehabilitation of illness in the senior citizens and elderly. Given the medical complexity of older adults, Geriatricians help make a difference in a senior citizen’s level of independence, well-being & quality of life.
I am currently the first MD of Geriatric Medicine in Rajasthan. After being trained in prestigious institute of AIIMS-Delhi for ~12 years (6 years as Geriatrician), I am committed to provide the highest quality of comprehensive medical care to senior citizens.
